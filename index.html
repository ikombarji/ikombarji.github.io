Ibrahim Kombarji is an architect, design researcher and writer based in Milan, Italy. ibrahimkombarji@gmail.com

His work explores the entanglements between architecture, art, ecology, and geopolitics currently with a strong focus on project-based collaborations in exhibition design and art direction. Ibrahim has previously worked with offices in Milan, London, Paris, and Beirut. His writings and interviews have been published in PIN-UP, Humboldt Books, and Errant Journal.
