Ibrahim Kombarji is an architect, design researcher and writer based in New York. ibrahimkombarji@gmail.com

His work examines the entanglements of architecture, art, ecology, and geopolitics. Currently, he focuses on project-based collaborations in exhibition design and art direction. Ibrahim has previously worked with offices in Milan, London, Paris, and Beirut. His most recent writings and interviews have been published in PIN-UP, Humboldt Books, and Errant Journal.
