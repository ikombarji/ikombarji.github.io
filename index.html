Ibrahim Kombarji is an architect, design researcher and writer based in Milan, Italy. ibrahimkombarji@gmail.com

His work explores the entanglements between design and geopolitics, looking at planetary realities relating to the atmosphere, the power-play at borders, and lately the ways in which carbon dioxide acts as a political device. He is currently working within Formafantasma. Ibrahim has previously collaborated with offices in New York, Paris, and Beirut.
